## SafetySpotIcon.png

This image is the standalone logo graphic for the application.

* **Shape & Style:** A polished, glossy 3D shield that conveys safety and protection.
* **Color Palette:** The shield is divided diagonally; the upper-left half is a bright gradient of blue and cyan, while the lower-right half is a vibrant lime green.
* **Central Element:** A smooth, stylized white ribbon or wave sweeps across the center, elegantly forming the letter **"S"**.
* **Details:** Two white, four-pointed star sparkles are placed on the shield surface-one in the blue section and one in the green section-giving the icon a clean, high-quality, and modern look.
