## SafetySpot_Anmelden.png

This is the initial onboarding and authentication screen of the mobile application.

* **Background:** Features a clean, soft gradient transitioning from a light pastel blue at the top to a faint mint green at the bottom.
* **Branding:** The application logo (`SafetySpotIcon.png`) is prominently centered in the upper half. Directly beneath it is the dark blue app title **SafetySpot** (with "Safety" in dark blue and "Spot" highlighted in green), followed by the slogan text: *"Lern. Sicher. Stark."*
* **Interactive Elements:** Three main action options are vertically stacked at the bottom:
* A solid green, rounded pill button labeled **"Anmelden"** (Login) in white text.
* An outlined white pill button with a green border labeled **"Registrieren"** (Register) in green text.
* A plain text link at the very bottom that reads **"Als Gast ausprobieren"** (Try as a guest) in dark blue.
